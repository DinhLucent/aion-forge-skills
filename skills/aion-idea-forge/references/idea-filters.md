# Idea Filters

Reject or downgrade ideas when they match these patterns:

- No evidence ID.
- No named actor or workflow.
- The idea is mostly a technology label.
- The MVP requires rewriting unrelated systems.
- The idea automates a high-risk decision without guardrails.
- The value can be achieved with a simpler queue, filter, report, or alert rule.
- The idea creates new data capture burden without clear payoff.
- The idea hides audit trail or makes accountability unclear.

Useful feature hypotheses usually reduce one of:

- time to notice
- time to decide
- time to recover
- repeated manual reconciliation
- false positives
- missed high-severity cases
- maintenance toil
- integration ambiguity
